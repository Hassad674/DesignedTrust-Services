# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want participation in this project to be a welcoming, respectful,
and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background
or identity. Contributors and maintainers commit to fostering an open
and friendly community.

## Our standard

This project adopts the **[Contributor Covenant, version 2.1][cc]** as
its Code of Conduct. The full, authoritative text — including the
expected behaviors, unacceptable behaviors, enforcement
responsibilities, scope, and enforcement guidelines — is maintained at:

> **https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/**

In short: be kind, assume good faith, give and accept constructive
feedback gracefully, and respect differing viewpoints. Behavior that
harasses, demeans, or excludes others is not tolerated, in project
spaces and in public spaces when representing the project.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may
be reported confidentially to the project maintainer at:

> **conduct@designedtrust.com** (or hassad.smara69@gmail.com)

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The
maintainer is obligated to respect the privacy and security of the
reporter of any incident, and will apply the enforcement guidelines
from the Contributor Covenant (correction → warning → temporary ban →
permanent ban) in determining the consequences of any violation.
